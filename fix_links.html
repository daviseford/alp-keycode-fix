Why do I need to use this?

ALP uses UTM codes to track how people arrive at their website. You don't need to understand UTM codes to use this, but here's a quick example. Imagine you've placed an ad in a magazine for your product.

You want to track the effectiveness of that ad. What's the easiest way to do that?

Coupons! Coupons are often little more than tracking programs. When a customer redeems a coupon, you know that he saw your product in SkyMall, because you've got a coupon code that only went to SkyMall.

UTM codes work the same way, but for links to websites. In our case, the Keycode is like a coupon code. This page will assist you in automatically assigning keycodes to links.

When you fill out the form above and click "Submit," the Keycode will be automatically inserted into the link - saving you valuable time.

Later, we can use this link to examine how traffic is entering our website - this helps us pinpoint high-performing emails.
